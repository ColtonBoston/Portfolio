Hello! My name is Colton Boston. I'm a front-end developer in Shreveport, LA who enjoys creating fast, functional and responsive websites.

I have a B.S. in Computer Science from Louisiana State University Shreveport. Also, I recently earned a front-end web development certification from Free Code Camp, and I'm currently working on data visualization. I really had fun designing and coding all of the projects to complete the certification, and I learned so much from them. You can view some of them below, while the rest can be found on my CodePen profile.

In my free time you can find me playing guitar or video games, watching American Ninja Warrior and mixed martial arts, or chilling out with my dog, Zoe.
